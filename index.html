Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Do I need to do eKYC every year?
Yes. To ensure up-to-date records, the government may require annual eKYC re-validation.

Q2. Can I complete eKYC on mobile?
Yes. Visit the official portal using any smartphone browser and follow the same OTP verification steps.

Q3. What happens if my eKYC fails?
Your application remains pending until you re-submit correct details. Update Aadhaar or bank information and retry.

Q4. Is biometric verification mandatory?
No. OTP-based eKYC is sufficient unless your biometric data mismatch occurs.
